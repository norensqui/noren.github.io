FAQ

Questions, answered

The things prospective clients usually want to know before reaching out.

How do you price engagements?

Either a fixed project fee or a monthly retainer, scoped and agreed up front. You always know what you're paying before we start — no hourly surprises.

How quickly can you start?

Usually within one to two weeks of an intro call, once we've agreed on scope and have access to your systems.

Do you replace our accountant or work alongside them?

Either works. We can run your finance function end to end, or support and level up your existing team and tools.

What tools do you work in?

QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, Excel and Google Sheets, Power BI, Tableau, plus SQL and Python for heavier data work. We adapt to your stack.

How do you handle data security?

Least-access permissions, encrypted file sharing, and signed NDAs on request. Your financial data is treated as strictly confidential.
